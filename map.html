How to read the site

A map-shaped story

The website is designed as a visual path rather than a simple document. Start at the Clocktower Tram Stop, follow the ticket into the Underground Miro Market, then climb to the Rooftop Garden above City Hall. The plot passages at the side of each page act like route transfers, guiding you from one part of the story to the next.

The three places are connected by recurring visual objects: the living ticket, tram rails, bells, city signs, and lantern light. Together, they make the site feel like a hidden transit map for a secret city.

The story begins where the ordinary city breaks open.
